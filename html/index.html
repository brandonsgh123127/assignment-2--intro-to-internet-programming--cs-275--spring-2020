Making White Rice

Please input the amount of ounces of rice you would like to make:

  1. Combine oz of rice with oz of water and oz of olive oil
  2. Bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat to the lowest setting
  4. Cook for about 18 minutes

For slightly al dente rice:

  1. Combine of rice with oz of water or broth and oz olive oil
  2. Bring to a boil and stir once to mix
  3. Reduce heat to low, cover with a tight-fitting lid and cook for 25 minutes
  4. Remove from heat and let stand for 5 minutes
  5. Fluff with a fork and serve

For softer rice:

Increase liquid by 4 oz and cook time by 5 minutes
